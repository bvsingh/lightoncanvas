Location Fix

Save, organise and share your favourite places

Location Fix keeps all the places that matter to you in one place. Drop a pin, add a photo and notes, organise by subject, route to any of them, and share them with other people and apps. Everything syncs across your devices with iCloud. Available on the App Store for iPhone and iPad.

Core Features

  • Easily place a pin on a map to store your favourite location

  • Add rich meta data (name, address, photo, notes, subject etc.) to any location

  • Share location and associated meta data with others

  • Route to your locations using either Apple Maps or Google Maps applications

  • Organize your locations by subject

  • Save location information from other applications

  • Available on the AppStore

What Location Fix Does

Touch and hold the map to drop a pin, or save your current location with a tap. Give each place a name, photo, notes, pin colour and a subject group, then find it again on the map or in the searchable, grouped list.

Key capabilities include:

  • Drop a pin or save your current location

  • Rich detail per location: name, address, photo, notes, subject and pin colour

  • Organise locations into subject groups

  • Route with Apple Maps, Google Maps or Waze

  • Share to Messages, WhatsApp, maps apps, and Tesla / Rivian

  • Save places shared from other apps — including geotagged photos

  • Automatic iCloud sync across iPhone and iPad
