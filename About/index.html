About LightOnCanvas

Efficient software design and a keen interest in photography are the driving forces behind LightOnCanvas.com. Our main specialty is in Mac OS and iOS platforms, but other platforms (Blackberry, Android) are also utilized. Our belief is that software must be well-designed from the very beginning so that the end result is an efficient, robust, and user friendly application that fully meets the requirements of the customer and provides a great user experience.
